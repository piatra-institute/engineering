# Data for Volume III, Chapter 7 (Acoustics)

- `sound_speed_materials.csv` reference sound speeds, densities, and
  characteristic impedances for common acoustic media (air, water,
  metals, building materials). Used in the propagation and impedance
  sections.
- `octave_spectrum.csv` octave-band SPL spectrum of a representative
  machine with the A-weighting offsets and resulting dB(A) band levels.
  Generated by `code/spl_octave.py`; consumed by
  `figures/fig-spl-octave.tex`.
- `atmospheric_absorption.csv` representative atmospheric absorption in
  dB/km versus frequency at two relative humidities. Consumed by
  `figures/fig-atmospheric-attenuation.tex`.
- `room_mode_count.csv` enumerated eigenmodes of the 5 x 4 x 2.5 m room
  with cumulative count and axial/tangential/oblique classification.
  Generated by `code/room_modes.py`; consumed by
  `figures/fig-room-modes.tex`.

All numbers are representative reference values; precise atmospheric and
oceanographic absorption depend on temperature, humidity, salinity, and
pressure through the standard models cited in the chapter.
