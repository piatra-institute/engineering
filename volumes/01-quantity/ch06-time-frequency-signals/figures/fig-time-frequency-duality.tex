\caption[Time-frequency duality]{The duration of a recording and
the spectral resolution of its transform are reciprocals. A long
rectangular pulse (top left) transforms to a narrow sinc-shaped
spectrum (top right). A short pulse (bottom left) transforms to a
broad spectrum (bottom right). Product $\Delta t \cdot \Delta f$
is bounded below by a constant of order unity; the constant
depends on the precise definition of width.}
\label{fig:vol01:ch06:time-frequency-duality}
