\caption[Patriot clock drift]{Cumulative time-conversion error in
the Patriot range-gate software, as reconstructed from the GAO
report \cite{acc:gao-patriot-1992}. The error grows linearly at
approximately $9.5 \times 10^{-8}$ per tenth-of-a-second tick,
reaching about $0.34 \,\si{\second}$ after 100 hours of continuous
operation. At the Scud's speed of $1{,}676 \,\si{\meter\per
\second}$ this corresponds to a $570 \,\si{\meter}$ predicted-
position error, larger than the range gate. The figure illustrates
the linear-accumulation pathology common to many long-running
counter-based clocks.}
