  -3/{grain of sand $\sim 10^{-3}\,\text{m}$},
  0/{person $\sim 10^{0}\,\text{m}$},

\caption[Order-of-magnitude scale ladder, length and mass]{Anchor
points for the working engineer's internal ruler across forty
decades of length and over fifty decades of mass. The figure is
read vertically: each decade is one factor of ten. A reader who
carries the left column in working memory can place any length
within an order of magnitude by interpolation between two anchors;
the same applies to the right column for mass. The ruler is built
by repeated use, not by memorisation; the table is the target, not
the start.}
