\caption[Two independent routes bracketing the truth]{Two
independent decompositions of the same quantity, refined step by
step. The bottom-up route (from component data upward) approaches
the truth from below; the top-down route (from an aggregate
allocation downward) approaches from above. The shaded band is the
factor-of-three working target. When the two routes enter the band
and agree, the estimate is reportable; while they straddle the band
from opposite sides, the gap between them is the honest uncertainty.
Routes that converge from the same side share a common assumption
and do not triangulate.}
