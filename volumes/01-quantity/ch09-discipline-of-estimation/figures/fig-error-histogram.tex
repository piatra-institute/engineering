\caption[Histogram of estimation errors for a population of fifty
Fermi problems]{The reader who has completed all fifty problems in
section~9.9 plots the histogram of $\log_{10}(\text{estimate}/
\text{truth})$ across the problems for which ground truth is
available. A well-calibrated Fermi practitioner produces a histogram
centred close to zero (no systematic bias) with about two-thirds of
the entries inside the factor-of-three band. Systematic bias shows
as a shift away from zero; an excess of large-magnitude errors
shows in the tails. The shape of the reader's histogram is the
chapter's quantitative self-assessment.}
