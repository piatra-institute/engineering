\caption[Variance budget of the Chicago piano-tuner estimate]{The
postmortem read as a budget. Each factor in the piano-tuner
decomposition contributes a share of the total log-variance
$\sigma_{\ln X}^{2}$, and the shares sum to one. A single factor here
carries almost half the budget, which is why the estimate's tightness
is set by that one factor and why sharpening any of the other three
buys almost nothing. The working rule the budget makes visible is that
effort spent narrowing a factor should be proportional to that
factor's share of the budget, not spread evenly across the
decomposition.}
