\caption[Calibration cost pyramid]{The calibration cost pyramid with
representative instrument prices and annual calibration cost at each
tier, current as of 2026. The top tier (NMI primary realisations) is
amortised across all users of an SI quantity; the bottom tier (field
and household instruments) accepts manufacturer specifications in
lieu of accredited calibration. Each tier inherits the uncertainty
of the tier above, multiplied by a factor depending on the transfer
discipline; each tier carries an annual cost in calibration services
that scales with both instrument capability and operational stakes.
Specific cited instruments are illustrative of their tier
\cite{web:ch03t3-keysight-34470a, web:ch03t3-fluke-5520a,
web:ch03t3-fluke-1551a-stik, web:ch03t3-microsemi-5071a,
web:ch03t3-microsemi-mhm-2010}.}
