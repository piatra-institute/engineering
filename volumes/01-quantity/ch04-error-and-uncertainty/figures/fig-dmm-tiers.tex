\caption[DMM tier cost-of-precision]{Acquisition cost versus DC-voltage
accuracy for the working tiers of digital multimeter and reference,
plotted on log-log axes. Each decade of accuracy roughly doubles
acquisition cost in the handheld-to-benchtop range; the cost increment
per decade rises steeply at the reference-instrument and primary-
realisation tiers. The Programmable Josephson Voltage Standard (PJVS)
at the National Institute of Standards and Technology realises the
volt to the quantum metrological floor at parts-per-billion uncertainty.
Cost figures are current as of 2024 list prices for the named
instruments. Cited from Vol I Ch 4.7 cost-of-precision worked case.}
