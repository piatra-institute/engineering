# Data assets — Volume VI, Chapter 12 (Biocompatibility and medical devices)

## Files

| File | Source / provenance | Used by |
|---|---|---|
| `bjork_shiley_fracture_by_size.csv` | Synthesised from the size-stratified rates reported in Blot, Ibrahim, Inglesby et al., Circulation 2005 (Table 2); fracture incidence per patient-year by valve size and opening angle. Used illustratively; specific rates carry uncertainty. | §12.7, exercises 22-23. |
| `depuy_asr_revision_rates.csv` | Cumulative revision-rate by year cohort, after the National Joint Registry of England and Wales 2010-2023 reports and the Australian Orthopaedic Association NJRR 2007-2023 reports; reproduces the trend, not the specific cohort counts. | §12.7, exercises 24-25. |
| `fda_recalls_by_class.csv` | Synthesised summary of recall counts by class (I/II/III) and by device category for a representative fiscal year. Shape representative of FDA MAUDE / recall database; specific numbers illustrative. | §12.6, exercise 4. |
| `iso_10993_test_matrix.csv` | The ISO 10993-1 (2018) test-matrix entries indexed by contact type and contact duration. Reproduces the matrix structure for pedagogical use; the official matrix is in the standard itself. | §12.1, exercises 1-2. |
| `mom_hip_ion_levels.csv` | Synthesised whole-blood cobalt and chromium concentrations from MoM hip cohort studies, indexed by acetabular cup inclination angle. Trend reproduced from Langton et al. 2011 and Cohen 2012. | §12.3, exercises 11-12. |

## Provenance and licensing

All data files in this folder are either (a) reproductions of public-domain regulatory data (FDA recall counts, registry annual reports) or (b) synthesised illustrative summaries based on the cited literature. No file is a verbatim extract from a copyrighted dataset; rates and shapes match the cited sources but specific row-by-row entries are pedagogical.
