# Data assets: Vol VI Ch 13

Living engineering: regenerative medicine, the anatomical compiler.

| file | provenance | what it is |
|------|-----------|------------|
| `planarian_regeneration_timecourse.csv` | synthetic, calibrated against published planarian regeneration phenomenology (Wenemoser & Reddien 2010; Oviedo & Levin 2010) | per-day fractions of blastema, head, and tail emergence over a 10-day regenerative course |
| `salamander_limb_regeneration_metrics.csv` | digitised range bounds from Goss 1969 (*Principles of Regeneration*), Stocum 2011 (*WIREs Dev Biol*), Tanaka 2016 (*Curr Top Dev Biol*) | typical days to blastema, digit emergence, and function across three urodele species |
| `bioelectric_voltage_measurements.csv` | range bounds from Hille 2001 (*Ion Channels of Excitable Membranes*), Adams & Levin 2007 (*Dev Cell*), Tseng et al.\ 2010, Pai et al.\ 2012 | typical resting potentials and intervention-induced shifts in published bioelectric morphogenesis assays |

All values are order-of-magnitude or range estimates synthesised from
the cited primary sources. None of these CSVs is a primary dataset.
The chapter uses them illustratively in estimation blocks; an
engineer evaluating a specific intervention should consult the
referenced primary papers and the species- and stage-specific
measurements they report.
