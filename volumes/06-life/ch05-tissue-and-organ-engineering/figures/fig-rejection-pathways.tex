\caption{Rejection mechanisms classified by time course (minutes-hours
hyperacute; days-weeks acute cellular; days-months acute
antibody-mediated; months-years chronic vascular), the dominant
effector at each scale (pre-existing antibodies; recipient T cells
recognising donor HLA; de novo donor-specific antibodies; chronic
vascular-wall fibrosis), and the engineering response built in at
the construct-design or clinical-protocol level. The autologous
iPSC-derived path avoids the rejection problem at the cost of months
of patient-specific cell preparation; the allogeneic path requires
HLA matching plus pharmacological immunosuppression.}
