# Chapter 7 — data assets

Reference datasets supporting Volume VI Chapter 7. All files are CSVs
with a single-line header and SI units explicit in the column names.

- `firing_rates_by_cell_type.csv` — typical baseline and peak firing
  rates by cortical cell class. Aggregated from Kandel \emph{Principles
  of Neural Science} 6th ed. (2021) ch. 23 and 26 with literature
  ranges.

- `ion_concentrations_nernst.csv` — intracellular and extracellular ion
  concentrations and the corresponding Nernst equilibrium potentials at
  310 K for a mammalian neuron. Source: Hille \emph{Ion Channels of
  Excitable Membranes} 3rd ed. (2001) table 1.1; Kandel ch. 6.

- `antibody_titer_postvax.csv` — IgM and IgG titre time course in
  arbitrary units after primary and booster vaccinations. Illustrative
  pattern based on Janeway \emph{Immunobiology} 9th ed. (2017) ch. 11.

- `mhc_binding_affinity.csv` — illustrative peptide-HLA binding
  affinities (Kd, nM) for HLA-A*02:01 (MHC class I) and HLA-DRB1*04:01
  (MHC class II). Values are illustrative magnitudes drawn from the IEDB
  range; specific peptides identified for instructional use only.
