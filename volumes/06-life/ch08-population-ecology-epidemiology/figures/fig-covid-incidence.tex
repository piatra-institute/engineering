\caption[COVID-19 first-wave case-incidence example]{Daily new

Zealand February--May 2020 in this illustration), with an SIR
Gaussian-envelope fit overlaid. The lockdown at day 35 corresponds
to a step reduction in $\beta$; the post-peak decay rate from day
50 onward gives the post-intervention $R_t$. The fit is
illustrative; production work belongs in the project deliverable.}
