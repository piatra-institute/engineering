\caption[Glycolysis pathway and ATP ledger]{The ten-enzyme glycolytic
pathway in the cytosol with the running ATP and NADH ledger. Steps 1
and 3 consume one ATP each (the investment phase); step 6 reduces two
NAD$^+$ to NADH; steps 7 and 10 produce two ATP each by substrate-
level phosphorylation (the payoff phase). The pathway nets $+2$ ATP,
$+2$ NADH, and $+2$ pyruvate per glucose. Enzymes: PGI = phospho-
glucose isomerase, PFK-1 = phospho-fructo-kinase 1, TPI = triose
phosphate isomerase, GAPDH = glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate dehydrogenase,
PGK = phosphoglycerate kinase, PGM = phosphoglycerate mutase.}
